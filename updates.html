What's Happening at True Grace?

A month-by-month summary

June 2024:

  • The vision of True Grace Ministries International is born with a two-fold mission:
    • To educate people in a proper understanding of the Bible, as interpreted in its original languages, with an emphasis on grace theology.
    • To train, equip, mentor and support pastors, missionaries and ministry leaders who will plant, lead, or be on staff at Biblically-sound, grace-oriented churches in the U.S. and across the globe.
  • True Grace Ministries International submits application for 501c3 non-profit status.
  • Founding board members are established: Bryan Threlkeld, Wendi Threlkeld, and David Uechi.

July 2024:

  • Jordan Davies commits to be our media director and create the first phase of our website/online presence.
  • True Grace Ministries signs a contract to partner with the Grace School of Theology and their ‘Grace on Demand’ training materials, providing True Grace Ministries with hundreds of hours of top-notch grace-oriented, biblical training materials, as well as access to their ministry certification program.
  • True Grace Ministries establishes their domain name, email, logos, and preliminary website. Our website is truegrace.online, and our email is: truegraceinfo@gmail.com.

August 2024:

  • True Grace Ministries receives their full 501c-3 status. Praise God!
  • True Grace Ministries establishes a checking account at First Texas Bank.
  • True Grace Ministries begins fund-raising efforts.
  • Executive Director, Bryan Threlkeld, leads an informational meeting for True Grace Ministries at Hill Country Bible Church Georgetown.
  • Executive Director, Bryan Threlkeld, casts the vision for True Grace Ministries with various individuals and small groups at Hill Country Bible Church Georgetown.
  • Plans are underway for creating a video/podcasting studio.

September 2024:

  • True Grace Ministries establishes their business address as PO Box 142, Salado, TX, 76571.
  • True Grace Ministries holds their official Fall 2024 Board Meeting.
  • Midtown Church in Brownwood, TX begins using our “Grace on Demand” materials for training, and they inform True Grace Ministries that they have 2–3 individuals who may wish to enter our video program.
  • Funding available for video/podcasting studio and work on the studio begins.

October 2024:

  • Penny Buchanan commits to oversee our accounting, finances and taxes.
  • Bryan T. leads a session on church-planting at the 2024 Free Grace Alliance (FGA) National Conference.
  • Bryan T. invited to speak on pastoral training and church-planting at the 2025 FGA National Conference.
  • Connections and partnerships are established between True Grace Ministries and other grace-oriented ministries, with the end-goal to provide more quality training materials on our website: truegrace.online.
  • A couple in Burnet, TX commits to fund our 2-year contract with the Grace School of Theology. A major praise and answer to prayer!

November 2024:

  • Website Phase 2 completed by Bryan Threlkeld and Jordan Davies.
  • Mark Wadsworth commits to do post-editing work for True Grace Ministries podcasts and videos.
  • The Association of Hill Country Churches commits to train future pastors using the same instructors and grace-oriented courses that we offer at True Grace Ministries.
  • Discussions are underway regarding Bryan Threlkeld and Pastor Danny Box possibly co-teaching a class on Free Grace Church Planting at the Grace School of Theology (a seminary in The Woodlands, TX).

December 2024:

  • Video/podcasting studio complete.
  • Bryan writing Biblical material and filming videos for our website, including:
    • A True Grace Ministries Overview video, explaining our vision and mission.
    • A podcast entitled: ‘Tipping Sacred Cows’, where we cover in-depth theological issues.
    • A ‘Bible Mysteries’ Video Series, where we tackle difficult to understand Bible passages in 5 minutes or less.
  • We begin exploring the possibility of a partnership with the Ephesus Biblical Training Center (Chris Morrison, Founder and Executive Director).
  • Year-to-date contributions (as of Dec 13, 2025) to True Grace Ministries International = $32,850.

Coming in January 2025:

  • ‘Tipping Sacred Cows’ weekly podcast release.
  • ‘Bible Mysteries’ video series bi-monthly release.
  • True Grace Ministries Winter Board Meeting.
  • Adding new, outstanding biblical/theological training resources on our website, including:
    • Bible & Theology Matters Podcast with Dr. Paul Weaver of Dallas Seminary.
    • Grace Life Ministries Resources - Dr. Charlie Bing.
    • The Free Grace Alliance resources.
    • Leading Grace Magazine.
    • Evantell Ministries.
    • Grace Walk Ministries.
    • Kids Talk About God resources.
    • Through the Bible Radio with Dr. J Vernon McGee.
